Questions parents ask

How does GameWatch know when my child is online?

We use public game APIs (like the Roblox Presence API) to check your child's online status. We only need their username — no passwords, no game account access, no installation on their device.

Does my child know they're being monitored?

GameWatch uses the same public status information any Roblox friend can see. There's nothing installed on your child's device and no way for them to detect it.

Can I monitor Fortnite and Minecraft too?

Roblox monitoring works now on all plans. Fortnite, Minecraft, Apex, and Valorant monitoring is available on Pro and rolling out through 2026.

Is my child's data safe?

We only store usernames and online/offline timestamps. We never access messages, friend lists, game content, or any private information. We never sell data.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel your Pro subscription anytime with one click. No contracts, no cancellation fees, no hassle.
